2. The Alphabet (Aëëni̱ Thuɔk Nath)

Nuer has special vowels that do not exist in standard English. Pay attention to the marks:

ä, a̱, ɛ, ɛ̈, ɛ̱̈, i̱, ɔ̱, ɔ, o̱, ö
